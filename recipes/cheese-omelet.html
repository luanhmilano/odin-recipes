Cheese Omelet

Description

That's an easy and cheap recipe with few ingredients, but is nutritional and delicious as an more expensive one.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 50g diced Tomato
  • 50g Green Pepper
  • 100g Mozzarella
  • Salt and Spice to taste

How to do

  1. Dice the tomato and the green pepper
  2. Grate the cheese
  3. Mix the eggs with salt
  4. Add the vegetables and the spices to the eggs in a bowl
  5. Heat a fry with butter
  6. Put the ingredients in the fry and let for 5 minutes each side
  7. Serve with rice
